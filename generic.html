Project « Citizen Council »

Lead a government project such as the "Citizens Council" set up in 2014 by the government (4 years project). This was a pilot project integrating a national mission. Citizen councils are, in France, independent structures of political power, with an objective, active and direct participation of all residents and neighbourhood actors, in the development and implementation of city contracts. Created advertising posters to recruit volunteers. I oversaw all the internal and external communication around this project. Created and lead the « Saint-Exupery citizen council » (Poissy 78). The deadlines for setting up this project were extremely short, but thanks to the volunteers, we did it on time. I waited for the completion of the project; "Independence of this citizen council and its recognition at the level of the city and the state"; before leaving my job and travelling.
